# PrintMD Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

## English

PrintMD does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Markdown files opened in PrintMD stay on your Mac. The app renders local documents for preview and printing, and it does not upload document contents to any server.

PrintMD does not require an account, does not include analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

If you contact the developer for support, the information you choose to provide will only be used to respond to that request.

## 中文

PrintMD 不收集、存储、出售或共享个人数据。

你在 PrintMD 中打开的 Markdown 文件会保留在你的 Mac 上。应用只在本机渲染文档以便预览和打印，不会把文档内容上传到任何服务器。

PrintMD 不需要账号登录，不包含分析或广告 SDK，也不会跨 App 或网站跟踪你。

如果你主动联系开发者寻求支持，你选择提供的信息只会用于回复该支持请求。
